About E-Cell VST

Building Tomorrow's Entrepreneurs Today

Who We Are

E-Cell VST is the Entrepreneurship Cell of Vedam School of Technology, dedicated to fostering innovation and entrepreneurial thinking among students. We believe that every student has the potential to create impact through innovative ideas and ventures.

Our Mission

To provide a platform where aspiring entrepreneurs can learn, network, and grow. We organize workshops, speaker sessions, competitions, and mentorship programs to help students transform their ideas into reality.

Workshops & Training

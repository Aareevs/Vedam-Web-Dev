My Adventures in the Mountains!



The Journey Begins!

Last summer, I decided to embark on an exciting adventure in the mountains. The fresh air and breathtaking views were absolutely worth the effort. It was so fun to just keep on climbing and occasionly look down to the amazing and mindblowing views. Truly an astonishing experience! The clean frozen lakes we saw occasionly, the clear skies and the ethereal snow made the experience so much better.



A Moment of Reflection.

During my journey,encountered various challenges and learned a lot about myself. I learnt about inner peace, how to take things slowly and appreciate life more. It was a truly unforgettable experience.



Hiking Essentials incase you want to go!

  • Thick and waterproof backpack
  • Leather jackets and thick cloth clothes
  • Hiking Boots
  • Spiked Walking Stick
  • Hot Water in Thermos
  • Canned Food

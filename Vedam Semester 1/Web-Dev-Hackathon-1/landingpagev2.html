Clue Pursuit: The Million Dollar Map

An Entrepreneurship Club Initiative | October 10th, 2:00 PM

Event Overview

Clue Pursuit is our annual campus-wide treasure hunt, meticulously crafted to test the agility, lateral thinking, and resourcefulness required to launch a successful startup. Each clue is a mini-business problem, a challenge in logistics, or a market research task. Teams must collaborate efficiently, manage their time under pressure, and occasionally, outbid their competitors to progress. This year's theme focuses on sustainable tech, requiring participants to think green while chasing the gold.

What to Expect:

  • Decryption Challenges: Puzzles requiring knowledge of basic financial metrics and coding logic.
  • Physical Tasks: Small, time-sensitive obstacles testing team coordination.
  • Networking Clues: Mandatory interaction with faculty mentors and alumni for specific information.
  • Grand Finale: A final 25 Question Quiz that will test everyone's emtreprenur thinking and skills.

The Prize Pool

Beyond the glory, massive incentives await the sharpest minds.

  • 1st Place: ₹1000 + 4 Books
  • 2nd Place: ₹500 Cash Prize
  • Registration: Free (Teams of 3-4)

Vedostav: Splendor of the Festival of Lights

Cultural Club Grand Celebration | October 11th, 6:00 PM

Cultural Highlights

An Unforgettable Night of Celebration Vedotsav is Vedam's signature Diwali celebration, where timeless tradition meets contemporary energy! This year, we lit up the campus with an unforgettable evening of high fashion, electrifying music, dazzling lights, and roaring laughter. It was a night for the entire VST family to connect, celebrate, and create brilliant memories together under the festive sky.

What to Expect:

  • Deepa Maala: A collective lighting of traditional lamps and firecrackers!.
  • Live Stage Performances: Featuring the best student talent in dance and poetry.
  • Grand Feast: Complimentary traditional dinner buffet for all attendees.
  • Rampwalk: Students showcase their style by walking down the red carpet.

Event Details

No registration is required for attendance, but volunteer sign-ups are open!

  • Attire: Traditional Indian Wear
  • Entry Fee: Free for Students & Staff
  • Volunteers: Report to Cultural Head by Oct 11th

BGMI Tournament: Zone-In For Glory

E-Sports Club Championship | September 15th

Tournament Format

This is the premier college esports event! The tournament spans three days, starting with qualifiers and culminating in the Grand Finals. The format is standard Battle Royale Squads (4v4), primarily played on the Erangel map. We prioritize fair play, and all matches will be streamed live on the Vedam Lecture Theatre TV, with professional commentary. Teams are encouraged to practice communication, strategy, and precision before the drop.

Key Rules:

  • Team Size: 4 players only.
  • Device: Mobile devices only (no emulators).
  • Fair Play: Strict rules against cheating, VPNs, and unauthorized modifications.
  • Match Schedule: Detailed schedule will be released one week prior to the event.

The Loot

Compete for the biggest prize pool in campus gaming history.

  • 1st Place: Gold Medals + Trophy
  • 2nd Place: Silver Medals
  • MVP Award: New Gaming Headset

Clue Pursuit: The Entrepreneur's Challenge

Hosted by the **Entrepreneurship Club**, Clue Pursuit is not just a treasure hunt—it's a high-stakes simulation of the startup world. Teams must decipher cryptic clues, navigate complex challenges, and apply basic business principles to reach the final prize.

• **Date:** October 28th

• **Venue:** Central Lawn to Incubation Centre

• **Prize:** ₹10,000 cash + Incubation Mentorship

Register for the Hunt

Vedostav: A Festival of Lights and Culture

Organized by the **Cultural Club**, Vedostav is our college's premier Diwali celebration. Immerse yourself in the vibrant traditions of India with spectacular dance performances, live music, a grand feast, and the beautifully decorated *Deepa Maala* (row of lamps).

• **Date:** November 10th

• **Venue:** Amphitheatre & Main Quad

• **Highlights:** Cultural Performances, Food Stalls, Sky Lanterns

BGMI Tournament: The Ultimate Battleground

The **E-Sports Club** presents the most anticipated gaming event of the year! Assemble your squad, sharpen your skills, and drop into the battle royale for a chance to prove you are the best. This is where legends are made and champions rise.

• **Date:** December 5th - 7th

• **Format:** Squads (4v4), Erangel Map

• **Total Prize Pool:** ₹25,000 + Gaming Gear

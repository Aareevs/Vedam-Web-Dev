Clue Pursuit: The Million Dollar Map

An Entrepreneurship Club Initiative | October 28th, 9:00 AM

Event Overview

Clue Pursuit is our annual campus-wide treasure hunt, meticulously crafted to test the agility, lateral thinking, and resourcefulness required to launch a successful startup. Each clue is a mini-business problem, a challenge in logistics, or a market research task. Teams must collaborate efficiently, manage their time under pressure, and occasionally, outbid their competitors to progress. This year's theme focuses on sustainable tech, requiring participants to think green while chasing the gold.

What to Expect:

  • **Decryption Challenges:** Puzzles requiring knowledge of basic financial metrics and coding logic.
  • **Physical Tasks:** Small, time-sensitive obstacles testing team coordination.
  • **Networking Clues:** Mandatory interaction with faculty mentors and alumni for specific information.
  • **Grand Finale:** A final pitch simulation where the winning team unveils the "treasure" – their solution to a real-world problem.

The Prize Pool

Beyond the glory, massive incentives await the sharpest minds.

  • 1st Place: ₹10,000 + Incubation Spot
  • 2nd Place: ₹5,000 Cash Prize
  • Registration: Free (Teams of 2-4)

Vedostav: Splendor of the Festival of Lights

Cultural Club Grand Celebration | November 10th, 6:00 PM

Cultural Highlights

Vedostav is the cultural heart of our college, celebrating the spirit and joy of Diwali. The evening begins with a traditional *aarti* ceremony followed by mesmerizing performances, including classical dances, folk music, and drama skits that depict historical and mythological tales. The entire quad area is illuminated with thousands of *diyas* and colorful string lights, creating a magical atmosphere. This is a day for the entire college community to come together, enjoy authentic Indian cuisine, and share the warmth of the festival.

What to Expect:

  • **Deepa Maala:** A collective lighting of over 1000 traditional lamps.
  • **Live Stage Performances:** Featuring the best student talent in dance, music, and poetry.
  • **Grand Feast:** Complimentary traditional dinner buffet for all attendees.
  • **Art Installation:** A collaborative rangoli and festive backdrop creation.

Event Details

No registration is required for attendance, but volunteer sign-ups are open!

  • Attire: Traditional Indian Wear
  • Entry Fee: Free for Students & Staff
  • Volunteers: Report to Cultural Head by Nov 1st

BGMI Tournament: Zone-In For Glory

E-Sports Club Championship | December 5th - 7th

Tournament Format

This is the premier college esports event! The tournament spans three days, starting with qualifiers and culminating in the Grand Finals. The format is standard Battle Royale Squads (4v4), primarily played on the Erangel map. We prioritize fair play, and all matches will be streamed live on the E-Sports Club's channel, with professional commentary. Teams are encouraged to practice communication, strategy, and precision before the drop.

Key Rules:

  • **Team Size:** 4 players (1 optional substitute allowed).
  • **Device:** Mobile devices only (no emulators).
  • **Fair Play:** Strict rules against cheating, VPNs, and unauthorized modifications.
  • **Match Schedule:** Detailed schedule will be released one week prior to the event.

The Loot

Compete for the biggest prize pool in campus gaming history.

  • 1st Place: ₹15,000 + Custom Jerseys
  • 2nd Place: ₹10,000 Cash Prize
  • MVP Award: New Gaming Headset
